Texas Maternal Health and Outcomes

The wellbeing of the mother, prior to her own birth, can determine the health of her pregnancy and child. Texas needs to implement efforts targeted at preventing and intervening in intergenerational health risks.

Parental Substance Use in the Child Welfare System

To reduce the number of children and families suffering from substance use, Texas must ensure access to and implementation of community-driven, cost-efficient and proven prevention, intervention, and treatment programs and services.

Preserving and Building Strong Families

Separating children from their biological family can be traumatic, often leaving lasting negative impacts. Texas can strengthen its family preservation services through implementing evidence-based services, thereby reducing reliance on the foster care system and other negative impacts.
